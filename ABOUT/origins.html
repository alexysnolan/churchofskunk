ORIGINS AND HISTORY

On a mild September evening, Skunk was recognized as a deity for the first recorded time by Jonny Lukins and Lex Nolan. The two were separated geographically (Jonny in Columbus, Ohio and Lex in Baldwin, Michigan), but they shared the spiritual experience of skunk seconds on Berd's YouTube channel. The two felt Skunk's blessings upon them as they were brought to tears by the beauty of SKunk's holy form.

As the last skunk second concluded, in a moment of divine inspiration, Jonny was compelled to create artistic depictions of skunk and the cat form of the Goddess Macha combined as one. With the creation of these holy images, the divinity of Skunk could no longer be denied. Thusly, on the Twelfth of September, in the year Two thousand and Twenty Four, the Church of Skunk was born. Jonny and Lex began to wish Skunk's holy blessings upon each other, as well as their friends, colleagues, and family members.

Interest in the church began to grow immediately; the first official convert was only 9 days later on September 21st. Since then, membership has continued to grow slowly but steadily and additional Deities have been added to the Church pantheon.

About this site

Lex launched the initial Church of Skunk website on May 25, 2025. Despite no former experience or training in web development, Lex's devotion to Skunk was so great that Skunk blessed her with enough ADHD hyperfocus powers to learn website coding and create this site. It is continually evolving and growing; Lex thanks coding wizard Sam D. for explaining in simple terms how websites work.
