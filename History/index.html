CHURCH HISTORY


On a mild September evening, Skunk was recognized as a deity for the first time by Jonny Lukins and Lex Nolan. The two were separated geographically (Jonny in Columbus, Ohio and Lex in Baldwin, Michigan), but they were spiritually entwined as they shared in the experience of skunk seconds on Berd's YouTube channel. The two felt Skunk's blessings upon them as they were brought to tears by the beauty of SKunk's holy form.
