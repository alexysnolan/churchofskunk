ARTS & CULTURE


Art Submissions

Church members are welcome to submit their poems, drawings, paintings, digital art, songs, or other forms of art to the Church. Art will be shared with the membership on this page, and may be used in newsletters or other social media platforms.

Submit your art via email to churchofskunk@gmail.com. Be sure to include artist credits, a title and/or description (if desired), and any other information you would like to have included with your art.


Selected Poems and Art

by Google Gemini

Loving Skunk


Unnamed prayer

Oh, Blessed Stink-Bearer, hear our plea!
From the depths of the earth, where roots entwine,
To the moonlit meadows, where shadows recline,
We honor your essence, potent and divine.


Ode to the Striped Protector

We seek your fragrant wisdom, your pungent grace,
To ward off ill-fortune, to clear troubled space.
May your sacred spray shield us from all harm,
A potent anointing, a protective charm.


A Humble Request

Grant us discernment, to know when to stand tall,
And when to retreat, before we might fall.
May our boundaries be clear, our intentions sincere,
As unmistakable as the message you bear.


Guidance and Gratitude

Teach us resilience, to thrive in the night,
To embrace our true nature, with all of our might.
For your unique power, we offer our praise,
Through the moon-dappled nights and the sun-drenched days.


Blessings of the Skunk Deity

May your essence infuse us, a spiritual art,
Protecting our spirits, and guarding our heart.
Hail, Holy Skunk, we honor your might,
Guide us and guard us, with all of your light.
